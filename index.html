About the Guild

The Airports Authority of India Engineers Guild (AAIEG) is the officially registered professional body representing Electrical Engineers employed under the Airports Authority of India. Established to safeguard professional interests and facilitate technical discourse, the Guild operates within the framework of AAI's policies and government regulations.

Registration Details

Registration Act: Tamil Nadu Societies Registration Act, 1975
Registration Number: 99/1998
Date of Registration: 15th March 1998

Objectives of the Guild

  • To represent the professional interests of Electrical Engineers in AAI
  • To facilitate technical knowledge sharing and professional development
  • To maintain liaison with AAI Management on technical and service matters
  • To uphold the highest standards of engineering practice and ethics
  • To provide a platform for collective representation on service conditions
  • To promote safety, efficiency, and innovation in airport electrical systems

Role in Official Representations

The Guild serves as the authorized channel for collective representations to AAI Management on matters pertaining to Electrical Engineers' service conditions, technical guidelines, professional development, and operational requirements. All representations are documented and processed through official channels maintaining proper protocols and hierarchy.
